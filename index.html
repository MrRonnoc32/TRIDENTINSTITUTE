Mission

Bridge the evaluation capacity gap for small and mid-sized nonprofits through equitable, transparent, subscription-based evaluation and data support.

Affiliation

Envisioned within Jacksonville University Public Policy Institute, with regional and national partners.

Why TRIDENT?

Rationale

  • High evaluation costs and shortage of trained evaluators create barriers for smaller nonprofits.
  • Grants increasingly demand rigorous evidence and metrics.
  • TRIDENT makes evaluation affordable and right-sized.

Geography & Policy Context

Northeast Florida focus: Duval, Clay, St. Johns, Nassau, Baker, Putnam, Flagler counties.

Aligned with federal, state, and local accountability requirements.

Scalable across Jacksonville, Tampa, Orlando, and Savannah markets.

Services

Strategy & Logic Models

Develop theory of change, PICOS, and indicator frameworks grounded in community realities.

Study Design & Analytics

Implement PDSA cycles, quasi-experimental methods, and multi-site RCTs tailored to your goals.

Data Architecture

Build clean data pipelines and reproducible analytics to power confident decision-making.

Partnership Development

Connect nonprofits with evaluators, universities, and partners for sustained learning networks.

Evaluation Capacity Studio
